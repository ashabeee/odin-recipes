Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Muffins

Description

The best part of fall

Ingredients

  • 1 large can of pureed pumpkin
  • 3 cups of sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup canola oil
  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups chocolate chips
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tbsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp vanilla

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F.
  2. In a large bowl, cream the oil an sugar together. Mix in the vanilla and pureed pumpkin
  3. In a medium bowl, mix all dry ingredients together. Add it to the creamed pumpkin mixture only until combined.
  4. Fill the muffin tins 2/3 of the way with the mixture.
  5. Bake for 30 mins and let cool at least 15 mins.
